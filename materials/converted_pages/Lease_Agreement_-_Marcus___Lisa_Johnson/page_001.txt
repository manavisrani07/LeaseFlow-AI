RESIDENTIAL TENANCY AGREEMENT
Residential Tenancies Act 1997 (VIC)
TENANCY SUMMARY

TERMS AND CONDITIONS OF TENANCY

This Residential Tenancy Agreement is entered into between
the Landlord and the Tenant(s) as identified in the Tenancy
Summary above. The Tenant(s) agree to rent the Premises
from the Landlord subject to the terms and conditions set
out below. Both tenants are jointly and severally liable
under this agreement.

Clause 1 — The Premises

The Landlord agrees to let, and the Tenant(s) agree to

rent, the residential premises located at:

18 River Road, Unit 7, Abbotsford VIC 3067

The Premises are to be used solely as a private residential
dwelling and for no other purpose. The Tenant(s) shall not
conduct any business, trade, or commercial activity from

the Premises without the prior written consent of the
Landlord.

Clause 2 — Rent

(a) Amount: The Tenant(s) shall pay rent of $2,650.00 AUD
per calendar month.

(b) Due Date: Rent is due and payable on the 15th day of
each calendar month, commencing on 15/04/2026.

(c) Method of Payment: Rent shall be paid by electronic
funds transfer or such other method as agreed in writing
between the parties.

(d) Arrears: If rent is not received within fourteen (14)

days of the due date, the Landlord may issue a notice to
vacate in accordance with applicable legislation.

(e) Rent Increases: The Landlord may increase the rent
during the term of this agreement only in accordance with
the Residential Tenancies Act 1997 (VIC), and must provide
no less than sixty (60) days' written notice of any

proposed increase.

Clause 3 — Bond / Security Deposit

The Tenant(s) shall pay a bond of $5,300.00 AUD prior to or
upon taking possession of the Premises on 15/04/2026. The
bond shall be lodged with the Residential Tenancies Bond
Authority (RTBA) in accordance with the Residential
Tenancies Act 1997 (VIC). The bond will be refunded at the
end of the tenancy, subject to any lawful deductions for
unpaid rent, damage beyond fair wear and tear, or other
amounts owed under this agreement.

Clause 4 — Term of Tenancy

(a) Fixed Term: This tenancy is for a fixed term of 12
months.

(b) Commencement: The tenancy commences on 15/04/2026.
(c) Expiry: The tenancy expires on 14/04/2027.

(d) Renewal: If neither party provides the required notice

to end the tenancy prior to the expiry date, the tenancy

will continue on a month-to-month basis under the same
terms and conditions.

(e) Early Termination: The Tenant(s) may terminate this
agreement before the end of the fixed term only in
accordance with the provisions of the Residential Tenancies
Act 1997 (VIC). Break fees or compensation may apply.
