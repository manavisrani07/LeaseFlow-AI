RESIDENTIAL TENANCY AGREEMENT
Residential Tenancies Act 1997 (VIC)
TENANCY SUMMARY

TERMS AND CONDITIONS OF TENANCY

This Residential Tenancy Agreement is entered into between
the Landlord and the Tenant(s) as identified in the Tenancy
Summary above. The Tenant(s) agree to rent the Premises
from the Landlord subject to the terms and conditions set
out below. Both tenants are jointly and severally liable
under this agreement.

Clause 1 — The Premises

The Landlord agrees to let, and the Tenant(s) agree to

rent, the residential premises located at:

18 River Road, Unit 7, Abbotsford VIC 3067

The Premises are to be used solely as a private residential
dwelling and for no other purpose. The Tenant(s) shall not
conduct any business, trade, or commercial activity from

the Premises without the prior written consent of the
Landlord.

Clause 2 — Rent

(a) Amount: The Tenant(s) shall pay rent of $2,650.00 AUD
per calendar month.

(b) Due Date: Rent is due and payable on the 15th day of
each calendar month, commencing on 15/04/2026.

(c) Method of Payment: Rent shall be paid by electronic
funds transfer or such other method as agreed in writing
between the parties.

(d) Arrears: If rent is not received within fourteen (14)

days of the due date, the Landlord may issue a notice to
vacate in accordance with applicable legislation.

(e) Rent Increases: The Landlord may increase the rent
during the term of this agreement only in accordance with
the Residential Tenancies Act 1997 (VIC), and must provide
no less than sixty (60) days' written notice of any

proposed increase.

Clause 3 — Bond / Security Deposit

The Tenant(s) shall pay a bond of $5,300.00 AUD prior to or
upon taking possession of the Premises on 15/04/2026. The
bond shall be lodged with the Residential Tenancies Bond
Authority (RTBA) in accordance with the Residential
Tenancies Act 1997 (VIC). The bond will be refunded at the
end of the tenancy, subject to any lawful deductions for
unpaid rent, damage beyond fair wear and tear, or other
amounts owed under this agreement.

Clause 4 — Term of Tenancy

(a) Fixed Term: This tenancy is for a fixed term of 12
months.

(b) Commencement: The tenancy commences on 15/04/2026.
(c) Expiry: The tenancy expires on 14/04/2027.

(d) Renewal: If neither party provides the required notice

to end the tenancy prior to the expiry date, the tenancy

will continue on a month-to-month basis under the same
terms and conditions.

(e) Early Termination: The Tenant(s) may terminate this
agreement before the end of the fixed term only in
accordance with the provisions of the Residential Tenancies
Act 1997 (VIC). Break fees or compensation may apply.
Clause 5 — Occupants

The maximum number of occupants permitted to reside at the

===== page_002.png =====
Premises is two (2). The named occupants under this
agreement are Marcus Johnson and Lisa Johnson. No
additional persons shall reside at the Premises without the
prior written consent of the Landlord.

Clause 6 — Pets

Pets are not permitted at the Premises without the prior
written consent of the Landlord. Any request to keep a pet
must be made in writing. The Landlord may grant or refuse
consent, and may impose reasonable conditions. Any approval
given for a pet may be revoked if the conditions are not
met.

Clause 7 — Parking

Parking is not included as part of this tenancy agreement.
The Tenant(s) shall make their own arrangements for vehicle
parking. The Tenant(s) must not park in any area designated
for other tenants, visitors, or emergency access.

Clause 8 — Tenant Obligations

The Tenant(s) agree to:

(a) Pay rent on time and in the manner specified in this
agreement.

(b) Keep the Premises in a reasonably clean condition and
not cause or permit damage beyond fair wear and tear.

(c) Not make any alterations or additions to the Premises
without the prior written consent of the Landlord.

(d) Not use the Premises or permit them to be used for any
illegal purpose.

(e) Not cause or permit any nuisance or interference with
the reasonable peace, comfort, and privacy of neighbours.
(f) Notify the Landlord or Managing Agent promptly of any
damage to the Premises or any defect requiring repair.

(g) Allow the Landlord or their agent reasonable access to
the Premises for inspections, repairs, and other purposes
permitted by law, subject to proper notice.

(h) Return the Premises at the end of the tenancy ina
condition consistent with fair wear and tear, having regard
to their condition at the commencement of the tenancy.
Clause 9 — Landlord Obligations

The Landlord agrees to:

(a) Ensure the Premises are in a reasonably clean condition
and fit for habitation at the commencement of the tenancy.
(b) Maintain the Premises in good repair, including
structural repairs and repairs to essential services
(heating, plumbing, electrical, and hot water systems).

(c) Comply with all applicable building, health, and safety
laws in relation to the Premises.

(d) Not interfere with the Tenant(s)' reasonable peace,
comfort, and privacy.

(e) Provide the Tenant(s) with at least twenty-four (24)
hours’ written notice before entering the Premises, except
in cases of emergency.

(f) Lodge the bond with the Residential Tenancies Bond
Authority (RTBA) within ten (10) business days of receipt.
Clause 10 — Special Conditions

No special conditions apply to this tenancy.

Clause 11 — Signatures

By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have
read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms and
conditions set out in this Residential Tenancy Agreement.
