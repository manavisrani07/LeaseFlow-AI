6. OCCUPANCY

7. PETS

The tenant has been granted permission to keep one (1)
domestic cat at the property, subject to the following
conditions:

7.1. The pet must be desexed and microchipped in accordance
with Victorian law.

7.2. The tenant is responsible for any damage caused by the
pet, including but not limited to carpet, flooring, doors,

and window furnishings.

7.3. The property must be professionally cleaned at the end
of the tenancy, including a flea treatment, at the tenant’s
expense.

7.4. The landlord reserves the right to withdraw pet
permission if the pet causes repeated disturbance to
neighbours or damage to the property.

7.5. No additional pets may be kept without further written
approval from the landlord.

8. PARKING

No designated parking is included with this tenancy.

9. TENANT OBLIGATIONS

9.1. Pay rent on time and in full.

9.2. Keep the property in a reasonably clean condition and
report damage or maintenance issues promptly.

9.3. Not make alterations without prior written consent.
9.4. Not cause nuisance or interference with neighbours.
9.5. Comply with all pet-related conditions specified in
Section 7.

9.6. Allow inspections with appropriate notice.

9.7. Return the property in the same condition as at
commencement, allowing for fair wear and tear.

10. LANDLORD OBLIGATIONS

10.1. Ensure the property is clean and fit for habitation
at commencement.

10.2. Maintain the property in good repair.

10.3. Not unreasonably interfere with the tenant’s quiet
enjoyment of the property.

10.4. Comply with all notice requirements under the Act.
11. SPECIAL CONDITIONS

Nil. No special conditions apply.

12. GENERAL PROVISIONS

This agreement is governed by the Residential Tenancies Act
1997 (Victoria).
