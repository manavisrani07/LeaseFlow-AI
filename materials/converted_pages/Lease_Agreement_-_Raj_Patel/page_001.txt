Part A — Particulars

Part B — Terms and Conditions

The following terms and conditions govern this tenancy
agreement. Where a conflict arises between Part A and Part
B, the particulars in Part A shall prevail.

B.1 Rent

B.1.1 The tenant shall pay rent in the amount of $1,150.00
AUD per fortnight, payable fortnightly in advance,
commencing on 1 April 2026.

B.1.2 Rent shall be paid by electronic funds transfer to

an account nominated by the landlord or property manager.
The tenant must retain proof of all rental payments.

B.1.3 If the rent due date falls on a weekend or public
holiday, payment shall be made on the last business day
preceding that date.

B.1.4 Rent shall not be increased during the fixed term of
this agreement except in accordance with applicable tenancy
legislation.

B.2 Bond

B.2.1 The tenant shall pay a bond of $4,983.34 AUD
(equivalent to four weeks rent, calculated from the
fortnightly rental amount) prior to or upon commencement of
the tenancy.

B.2.2 The bond shall be lodged with the Residential
Tenancies Bond Authority (RTBA) in accordance with the
Residential Tenancies Act 1997 (Vic).

B.2.3 The bond, or any part thereof, shall be refunded to
the tenant at the end of the tenancy subject to the
premises being returned in a satisfactory condition,
reasonable wear and tear excepted.

B.3 Term

B.3.1 The tenancy shall commence on 1 April 2026 and
expire on 31 March 2027, being a fixed term of twelve (12)
months.

B.3.2 Should the tenant wish to continue the tenancy
beyond the fixed term, the parties may negotiate a new
agreement or the tenancy may continue on a month-to-month
basis in accordance with applicable legislation.
