5. BOND (SECURITY DEPOSIT)

The bond shall be lodged with the RTBA within 10 business
days of receipt. The bond will be refunded at the end of
the tenancy subject to the property being returned in a
satisfactory condition, allowing for fair wear and tear.

6. OCCUPANCY

The tenant must not allow any additional persons to reside
at the property without the prior written consent of the
landlord or managing agent.

7. PETS

Pets are not permitted at the property. The tenant must not
keep any animals at the premises without the prior written
consent of the landlord.

8. PARKING

No designated parking space is included with this tenancy.
Street parking may be available subject to local council
regulations.

9. TENANT OBLIGATIONS

9.1. Pay rent on time and in full by the due date each
month.

9.2. Keep the property in a reasonably clean condition and
notify the landlord or agent promptly of any damage or need
for repairs.

9.3. Not make any alterations or additions to the property
without the prior written consent of the landlord.

9.4. Not cause or permit any nuisance or interference with
the reasonable peace, comfort, or privacy of neighbours.
9.5. Allow the landlord or agent to enter the property for
inspections with appropriate notice as required under the
Residential Tenancies Act 1997.

9.6. Return the property at the end of the tenancy in the
same condition as at the commencement, allowing for fair
wear and tear.

10. LANDLORD OBLIGATIONS

10.1. Ensure the property is in a reasonably clean
condition and fit for habitation at the commencement of the
tenancy.

10.2. Maintain the property in good repair throughout the
tenancy, including structural repairs and maintenance of
essential services.

10.3. Not interfere with the reasonable peace, comfort, or
privacy of the tenant.

10.4. Comply with all notice requirements under the
Residential Tenancies Act 1997 for inspections, repairs, or
entry to the property.

10.5. Lodge the bond with the RTBA within the required
timeframe.

11. SPECIAL CONDITIONS

No special conditions apply to this agreement.

12. GENERAL PROVISIONS

This agreement is governed by the Residential Tenancies Act
1997 (Victoria) and any regulations made under that Act.
Any provision of this agreement that is inconsistent with
the Act is void to the extent of the inconsistency.
